<h1>
Keyboard Reference
</h1>
<p>
The following keyboard commands are supported:
</p>
<h2>
Movement
</h2>
<p>
Numpad+2 - moves one step to the south
</p>
<p>
Numpad+4 - moves one step to the west
</p>
<p>
Numpad+8 - moves one step to the north
</p>
<p>
Numpad+6 - moves one step to the east
</p>
<p>
Enter - enters a place, climbs a ladder/stairs
</p>
<h2>
Overview Commands
</h2>
<p>
<b>I</b>nventory - opens up the inventory dialog
</p>
<p>
<b>Z</b>stats - opens up the stats dialog - like in Ultima 2
</p>
<p>
<b>W</b>ear/Wield - opens up the equipment dialog - supposed to look like Ultima 6
</p>
<p>
<b>B</b>ook - opens up the Spellbook to pick a new spell
</p>
<p>
S<b>K</b>illtree - Skilltree
</p>
<p>
Alt+<b>O</b>ptions - Options Dialog
</p>
<h2>
Action Commands
</h2>
<p>
<b>G</b>et - Get something
</p>
<p>
<b>D</b>rop - open the Drop Dialog
</p>
<p>
<b>T</b>alk - Talk to someone
</p>
<p>
<b>M</b>ove - Move to
</p>
<p>
<b>S</b>earch - Search the surroundings
</p>
<p>
<b>A</b>ttack - attack someone
</p>
<p>
<b>L</b>ook - Look at something
</p>
<p>
<b>P</b>ush - Push something
</p>
<p>
<b>Y</b>ank - Yank(Pull) something towards you
</p>
<p>
<b>C</b>ast - Cast the currently active spell
</p>
<p>
<b>U</b>se - Use something - needs to be implemented properly
</p>
<p>
<b>O</b>pen - Open/Close a door
</p>
<h2>
Misc keys
</h2>
<p>
ESC - Exit current command
</p>
<p>
Space - wait one turn
</p>
